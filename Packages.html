Italian Delight

One of the pearls of Southern Italy, Sicily is the largest of the Italian islands, just off the "toe" of Italy's "boot." Sicily is rich in UNESCO World Heritage Sites: the archeological area of Agrigento, the famous Valley of the Temples, and Syracuse with the Necropolis of Pantalica. On Sicily’s eastern edge is Mount Etna, one of Europe’s most active volcanoes. Each corner and cranny of the island evokes charm and history, and is surrounded by green olive groves, almond trees, citrus orchards, and vineyards that stretch out to the sea. Package includes hotel with Hotel Villa Athena. and a rental car from Sixt.
Price $2000 per person.
